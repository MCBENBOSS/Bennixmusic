About MC BEN

MC BEN is a vibrant Afrobeat artist from Cross River State, Nigeria. He brings infectious rhythm, powerful messages, and high-energy performances to the world stage. Known for creating hits that connect with people across genres, MC BEN is committed to sharing his music and culture globally.

Follow his journey for the latest releases, freebeats, mixtapes, and exclusive videos. MC BEN is not just an artist, he’s a movement, representing Cross River State with pride.
